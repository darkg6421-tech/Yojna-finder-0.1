About Helper Guide

We believe accessing government services shouldn't be confusing or intimidating.

Helper Guide was started by documentation specialists who saw how many Indians struggle with Aadhaar updates, PAN corrections, and certificate applications. Our mission is to provide clear, step-by-step guidance in simple English.

We have assisted students, job seekers, and families from rural and urban areas alike. Every piece of information is verified and updated regularly.

Our principles

  • Clarity: No government jargon.
  • Trust: We never ask for sensitive data.
  • Accessibility: Works on any device.
